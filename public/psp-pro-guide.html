üìÜ Set Your Availability Coach ‚ñ∂
1 Go to Admin ‚Üí Availability.
2 Pick a service (or "Any Service" for general availability).
3 Choose a date, set start/end time, and enter the location.
4 Set max bookings per slot (e.g., 1 for individual, 8 for group).
5 Click "Create Slot". Athletes will now see this slot when booking.
You can only see and manage your own slots. Delete a slot anytime if plans change.
üèÉ Manage Athletes Coach ‚ñ∂
1 Go to Admin ‚Üí Athletes to see all athletes.
2 Each athlete card shows their sessions, drills, average velocity, and peak velocity.
3 Search by name/email or filter by sport.
4 Click "Add Athlete" to create a new athlete ‚Äî enter name, email, sport, and age.
5 For athletes under 18, parent/guardian fields appear automatically.
6 Click an athlete to see their detailed stats page and assign drills.
üìã Manage Bookings Coach ‚ñ∂
1 Go to Admin ‚Üí Bookings.
2 Toggle between Calendar View (monthly overview with color-coded dots) and Table View (detailed list).
3 In Calendar View, click a day to see all sessions for that date.
4 Use the status filter: All / Pending / Confirmed / Cancelled.
5 For pending bookings: click Confirm or Cancel.
6 For confirmed bookings: click Mark Complete after the session.
Status colors ‚Äî Green: confirmed, Yellow: pending, Red: cancelled, Blue: completed.
üéØ Create & Manage Drills Coach ‚ñ∂
1 Go to Admin ‚Üí Drills.
2 Click "Create Drill" and fill in: title, description, video URL, thumbnail, category, difficulty, and duration.
3 Toggle Published on to make it visible to athletes.
4 To add drills in bulk, go to Admin ‚Üí Drills ‚Üí Import and upload a CSV file.
5 To assign drills to a specific athlete, go to their athlete detail page and select drills.

üí∞ Manage Services & Pricing Admin ‚ñ∂
1 Go to Admin ‚Üí Services.
2 Click "Add Service" ‚Äî enter name, description, price, duration, category (individual/group/package/specialty), and max participants.
3 To feature a service on the homepage, click the star icon. Up to 6 services can be featured.
4 Toggle services active/inactive ‚Äî inactive services won't appear in booking.
5 The Pricing page updates automatically ‚Äî it pulls directly from your services in the database.
Training packages (5/10/20-packs) are managed separately in the training_packages table. Contact your developer to update package pricing.
üí≥ Stripe & Payments Admin ‚ñ∂
1 When athletes book and pay, Stripe handles checkout securely. You don't need to touch payments manually.
2 After successful payment, a booking is created automatically and appears in your Bookings page as "Pending".
3 View payment details and revenue stats in the Bookings dashboard and Analytics section.
4 To manage refunds, disputes, or payment settings, log into your Stripe Dashboard directly.
